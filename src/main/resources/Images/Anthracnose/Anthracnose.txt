Chemical controlKarnataka1. Carbendazim/ Difenconazole or Thiophanate methyl at 0.1% or Chlorothalonil 0.2% sprays at fort-nightly intervals have been found effective.2. Spraying of Difenconzole 25 EC at 0.25ml/lt or Prochloraz 45 EC at 0.75ml/lit were effective against anthracnose disease.(UAS, Dharwad)3. Spraying of systemic fungicides namely Hexaconazole (0.1%) / Thiophanate methyl (0.1%)/ Carbendazim (0.1%) were most effective. Among the contact fungicides Chlorothalonil (0.2%) followed by Mancozeb (0.2%) were more efficacious.
